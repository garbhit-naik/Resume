Profile
I am an interdisciplinary researcher and social designer with a background in architecture, spatial data science, and urban sustainability. My work focuses on climate resilient built environment and co-creating recovery frameworks for highly vulnerable contexts. With experience across academic, grassroots, and international research collaborations, I specialize in mixed-methods analysis, spatial modelling, and community-based approaches. Through long-term engagements, I have developed a strong commitment to inclusive climate adaptation, sustainability transitions and decision-making under climate uncertainty
